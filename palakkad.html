Top Tourist Destinations of Palakkad

Palakkad, or Palghat, is a city in Kerala, a state in southwestern India. The 18th-century Palakkad Fort has sturdy battlements, a moat and a Hanuman temple on its grounds. North, on the Kalpathy River, the 15th-century Viswanatha Swamy Temple is the main venue of the famous Ratholsavam chariot festival. Northeast, near Malampuzha Dam, the town of Malampuzha has a rock garden created from recycled materials. This beautiful district in Kerala is very famous for the Palakkad Fort, the Jain temple, the annual Ratholsavam and of course for the extremely beautiful green and lush Malampuzha gardens and dams
